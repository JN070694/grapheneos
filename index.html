Installation:

  • Make sure connected to PC, not outlet.
  • Make sure a high quality, data transferring cable is used.

System Settings:

  • Sound and Vibration: Vibration & Haptics > OFF
  • Display: Lockscreen: Tap & Lift to Check > OFF
  • Display: Dark theme > ON
  • Battery: Charging Optimization > ON
  • Developer options: Enable freeform windows > ON

Setting Preferences:

  • System > Users > (User List)
  • Security & Privacy > Device Unlock > Durress Password > ####
